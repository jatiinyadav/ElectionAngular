Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD) Elections - 2022

Ward: Kapashera

AAP Candidate - Aarti Yadav has won in Ward No. 132 Kapashera, which is reserved for Women Ward in MCD.

BJP Candidate - Sunita Yadav

INC Candidate - Laxmi Devi

Trump Card of Aam Aadmi Party in MCD Elections

AAP used the "House Tax" as its trump card. Despite the fact that property taxes account for 26 percent to 30 percent of the revenue generated by the three municipal corporations, AAP has vowed to abolish the entire system and waive off all pending dues. Along the House Tax card, The electricity bill scheme has helped majority of the population in saving their hard earned money when the electric units consumed is less than 200.
