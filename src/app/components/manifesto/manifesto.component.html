BJP Manifesto Sub-Points

Animal welfare/Stray animals This is a summary of the content

1. Monkeys will be moved from residential colonies and taken to protected forests

2. Measures will be taken to reduce the population of stray dogs and strays roaming on the streets will be sent to animal homes

 Budget Currently I am {{panelOpenState ? 'open' : 'closed'}}

1. Directly transferring funds to Delhi Municipal Corporations. If Delhi government does not transfer funds, central government will transfer the funds and the amount will be deducted from the state government's budget

Buildings & Housing Currently I am {{panelOpenState ? 'open' : 'closed'}}

1. On plots less than 105 square metres, maps will not need to passed. For plots less than 500 metres, there will be no compulsion to pass maps.

2. Solar energy plants will be installed and buildings will be encouraged to use solar energy

3. Delhi government will be pressurised to legalize unauthorised colonies

4. Dairy colonies will be developed for easy availability of milk and milk products for citizens.

5. Rainwater harvesting will be implemented

 Environment/pollution Currently I am {{panelOpenState ? 'open' : 'closed'}}

1. Special trees will be planted to reduce air pollution

 Garden/open spaces Currently I am {{panelOpenState ? 'open' : 'closed'}}

1. World-class gardens will be created and will be equipped with spaces for children to play

 Municipal School Education Currently I am {{panelOpenState ? 'open' : 'closed'}}

1. Smart classrooms will be made Municipal schools and provision for world-class education will be made. Study of English and computers will be made compulsory.

2. 'Adoption scheme' will be started through partnership of private schools and Municipal schools

3. Health insurance scheme for Municipal students

 Licensing and Hawking Currently I am {{panelOpenState ? 'open' : 'closed'}}

1. Single window system for all licences with minimum documents.

2. Small industries to be exempted from factory licences

3. Hawkers will be registered under the scheme of 'Security of hawkers rules, 2014 so that they can get loans easily without mortgage

4. All services will be provided online
