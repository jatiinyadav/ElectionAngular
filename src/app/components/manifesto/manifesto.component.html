AAP MANIFESTO SUB-POINTS

1. Financial Problems - AAP has loudly proclaimed to make MCD profitable within a year of its takeover. 2. Healthcare - AAP has vowed to free all tests and medications in MCD clinics, hospitals, and Mohalla clinics. 3. CM Kejriwal also promised to repair broken roads in the city. 4. In a bid to provide relief to the traders, CM Kejriwal promised to scrap conversion charges. 5. The AAP has also promised vending zone for vendors, assuring them they won't have to pay bribes. 6. All MCD employees will get their salaries on time, CM Kejriwal promised. 7. Most importantly, CM said the AAP will clear three landfill sites in the city.
